About Us

Our Goal

Our main goal is to connect Small Medium Enterprises (SMEs) from Spain and whole European countries in finding cooperation opportunities with African Companies to promote the economic growth in rural areas based on SDGs

Our Mission

Our mission is to create value for SMEs, shareholders, People with vulnerabilities and community.

We strive to be among the best providers of financial services and products in Africa, delivering a diverse range of established and pioneering products.

We are a highly talented workforce, committed to reliability and consistency, and maximizing every investment opportunity.

We believe in creating an environment where people want to work, and where personal success is aligned with company success.

Our Vision

We envision that financial inclusion can enable poor households to generate income, smooth consumption, protect against shocks, build asset and sustain live hoods.
